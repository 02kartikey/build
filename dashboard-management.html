📋 Recommended Actions
Management can take these steps to improve completion rates
✅ Weekly Progress Review
Hold a 15-min check-in with counsellors each Monday. Ask each school to share their current completion rate and blockers.
📢 School Assembly Announcement
Ask principals to make a 2-minute announcement encouraging students to log in and complete their assessment. Peer pressure works.
📞 Parent SMS / Notification
For schools below 60%, coordinate with the principal to send a parent SMS reminder about the assessment deadline.
🖥 Dedicated Lab Session
Organise a supervised computer lab session for not-started students. Most complete the assessment in one 60-minute sitting when given dedicated time.
